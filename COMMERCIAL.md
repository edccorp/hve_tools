## What EDC provides to HVE customers

EDC distributes and maintains HVE Tools as part of the HVE product family.
Access to the **official distribution, updates, and support** is provided to
licensed HVE users as part of their purchase and continued support /
maintenance fee. An active support agreement is what entitles you to:

- Official, tested builds of HVE Tools that match your HVE version.
- Updates, bug fixes, and new features as they are released.
- Support from EDC for installation and use with HVE.

The support / maintenance fee pays for this official channel and for support —
**not** for the software's copyright grant, which is GPL. This is the same model
used by many commercially maintained GPL projects: the code is free, and
customers pay for the maintained, supported, official product.
